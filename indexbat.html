About this archive

Structural archive of what shipped in the recovered client. Seven container formats fully decoded, every static asset surfaced. Combat numerics (HP, levels, damage, drops, XP) have not been found in the recovered client corpus and were likely server-authoritative on Sigma's now-dark servers. See About for the full breakdown of what's recoverable and why.

Reverse-engineered asset archive of Tales of Eternia Online (Namco / Sigma, 2003–2007). Static structural data extracted from the original client; no copyrighted asset binaries are redistributed here. Numeric gameplay values (HP, levels, drops, XP) were server-authoritative and lost when Namco's servers shut down — they are not part of this archive.
